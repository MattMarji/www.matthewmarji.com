What is TapInto?

TapInto Banner

TapInto takes advantage of NFC and Bluetooth LE Technology to provide new, interactive experiences at events such as career fairs, and seminars.

Our technology will allow users to exchange data with a simple tap, find other events nearby, and access all event content right from their smartphone.

Current Development!

TapInto is being developed on all fronts as we speak!
The back-end team is completing the login process for users using our web-app and mobile app.

Security means a lot to us, so we are spending extra time making sure no one can get into our servers!

Our Mobile Team is hard at work creating a beautiful front-end and making sure the app flawlessly interacts with our back-end that is being built with PHP, Ruby on Rails, and a bit of GO.
